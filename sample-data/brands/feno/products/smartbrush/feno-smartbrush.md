# Feno Smartbrush Product Overview

**Description (what is it?)**
The Feno Smartbrush is a revolutionary oral health platform that combines a U-shaped mouthpiece toothbrush with built-in AI-powered oral scanning technology. It's the world's first consumer toothbrush that simultaneously delivers a comprehensive 20-second full-mouth clean while monitoring oral health and detecting early signs of gum disease, tooth decay, and systemic health risks. Unlike traditional electric toothbrushes that require 2 minutes of manual technique or basic U-shaped brushes that only clean without monitoring, Feno creates an entirely new product category: the "health monitoring smartbrush." The system includes a Smarthandle with built-in oral scanner, personalized TrueFit mouthpiece sized to individual dental arches, magnetic wireless charger, and the Feno Plus app that provides AI-powered health insights linking oral health to heart disease, diabetes, and cancer risk. The core value proposition is transforming a daily 20-second routine into a comprehensive disease prevention platform that saves 21 hours per year while providing continuous health monitoring previously only available at dental offices.

**Unique Mechanism (how does it work?)**
1. **Personalized Sizing**: User measures dental arch using app-guided process; Feno ships correctly-sized TrueFit mouthpiece for optimal fit and coverage
2. **Simultaneous Full-Mouth Cleaning**: User applies Feno Foam (foaming toothpaste), inserts U-shaped mouthpiece, activates 20-second cycle. 18,000 nylon bristles vibrate in slow circular motion with 250 brush strokes, cleaning all teeth simultaneously via ArcFlow Tech arrangement
3. **AI Oral Scanning**: Built-in medical-grade camera in smarthandle scans teeth, gums, tongue, and soft tissue during or after brushing. Captures high-resolution images of oral cavity
4. **Machine Learning Analysis**: AI algorithms process scan images, identifying inflammation markers, plaque buildup, decay indicators, gum recession, and soft tissue abnormalities. Compare to historical data to detect changes and trends
5. **Health Insights Generation**: App translates analysis into actionable insights: oral health score, area-specific feedback (e.g., "inflammation detected in lower left molars"), systemic health correlations (e.g., "gum inflammation linked to cardiovascular risk"), and personalized recommendations
6. **Continuous Monitoring Loop**: Each use adds data point; over time, AI learns user's baseline and detects deviations. User receives alerts for concerning trends, shares data with dentist for informed visits

**Manual (how do you use it?)**
1. **Daily Use (Brushing)**:
   - Dispense Feno Foam (pea-sized amount) onto mouthpiece bristles or directly in mouth
   - Insert U-shaped mouthpiece over teeth (upper or lower arch)
   - Press power button to start 20-second cleaning cycle
   - Allow bristles to vibrate and clean all surfaces automatically
   - Remove mouthpiece and rinse mouth with water
   - Repeat for opposite arch (upper/lower) for complete clean
   - Rinse mouthpiece under water and place on charger
   - Total time: 20 seconds per arch (40 seconds for both upper and lower)

2. **Health Monitoring (Scanning)**:
   - Open Feno Plus app on smartphone
   - Select "Scan Now" in app
   - Point smarthandle camera at teeth and gums following app guidance
   - App captures images automatically during 30-second guided scan
   - AI processes images and generates health report within 60 seconds
   - Review oral health score, trend graphs, and area-specific insights
   - Share data with dentist via app export feature (optional)
   - Recommended frequency: Daily after brushing or weekly for trend tracking
